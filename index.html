Social-Arena

Hi everyone! Welcome to Social Arena open-source research group.

What is Social Arena?

Social Arena is a cutting-edge AI-powered social media simulation platform for researching social dynamics, recommendation algorithms, and viral propagation at scale. We combine LLM-driven agents, Twitter-style data models, and sophisticated recommendation systems to simulate realistic social networks.

üéØ Core Architecture

  • Agent - AI agents with 9 fundamental social actions (post, reply, like, follow, etc.)
  • Feed - Twitter-style Pydantic data models for posts and content
  • Recommendation - Twitter-algorithm-inspired centralized recommendation system
  • Arena - Integration layer orchestrating multi-agent simulations

Why Social Arena?

In recent months, AI applications have made remarkable strides across engineering (Cognition Labs, Cursor), video creation (Pika, Runway), and specialized domains. Yet social-centric industries remain relatively underserved.

We aim to change that by launching a permissively open-source research initiative uniting academic and public efforts across AI, HCI, social sciences, and beyond. Our goal: produce high-quality research worthy of publication at top-tier AI conferences while maintaining complete transparency.

Quick Start

# Install from GitHub
pip install "arena @ git+https://github.com/Social-Arena/Arena.git@dev"
# Or clone and develop locally
git clone https://github.com/Social-Arena/Arena.git
cd Arena
git submodule update --init --recursive
pip install -e .
