Social-Arena

Hi everyone! Welcome to Social Arena open-source research group.

In recent months, AI applications have made remarkable strides, promising to unlock unprecedented levels of human creativity and productivity—while driving improvements across engineering, healthcare, education, entertainment, and beyond.

Unicorns able to deliver early values have emerged, with Cognition Labs (now valued at 10.2B), Cursor (valued at 9.9B) helping software engineers, Pika (valued at 4B), Runway (valued at 3B), Luma (valued at 3.2B) helping video creators, Mercor (valued at 10B) for human resources specialists, Harvey (valued at 5B) for lawyers, Clay (valued at 3.1B) for go-to-market, Chai (1.2B) for biologists, Axiommath for mathematicians, Moonlake for game developers, Utopai for film-makers, Shortcut and Maximor for financial analysis, ListenLabs for customer support, not to list all of them.

Here, we hope to contribute to the social-centric industries.

True to our open-source philosophy, all deliverables will be public from day one. We will not maintain any private repositories under our GitHub organization, ensuring full transparency and attribution. Every contributor will receive due credit, and we collectively aim to produce work of the highest quality—worthy of publication at top-tier AI conferences.

Research Focus

Our research investigates four fundamental dimensions of social media dynamics:

Scale Effects
How agent numbers impact network formation

Activity Dynamics
Posting frequency effects on engagement

Network Evolution
Long-term stability and behavior patterns

Algorithm Effects
Recommendation strategy comparisons
